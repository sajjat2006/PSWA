About Us

Painshail Student Welfare Association (PSWA) is a volunteer-driven organization founded by the youth of Painshail with a simple but powerful mission: to uplift, support, and empower students in our community. We believe that education is a fundamental right and that no student should be left behind due to financial or social challenges.

Since our formation, PSWA has been actively working to bridge gaps in educational access by organizing academic support programs, distributing essential school supplies, offering guidance for higher education, and creating a network of peer mentors.

We operate purely on a volunteer basis, driven by compassion, commitment, and a shared sense of responsibility. Every step we take is guided by the belief that when we uplift one student, we uplift the whole community.
